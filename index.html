How to play: Memo-flag is a memory card game with flags in which all of the cards are laid face down on a surface and two cards are flipped face up over each turn. The object of the game is to turn over pairs of matching cards. Move your mouse the the card you want to flip. Use left mouse click to flip the card. Do it again with another unflipped card. If the two flipped card is matched (the two cards have the same image), they will remain flipped with a green border (correct matched). Otherwise the cards border turn red (not matched). To win the game player needs to find all the pair of matching cards
